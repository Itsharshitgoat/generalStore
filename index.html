About Me

I’m a tech enthusiast on a quest to make AI and OS development less of a headache and way cooler. I tinker with modern tech, treat UI/UX like both art and sport, and basically run on wireframes and caffeine. Obsessed with futuristic design and buttery-smooth experiences, I try to make tech feel like magic—with less smoke and more logic. I’m curious, dangerously detail-oriented, and fueled by Wi-Fi and bad puns, always asking: “What if we made it do this… but cooler?”

I believe in the power of simplicity—because if a button needs a manual, we’ve already lost the plot. Every pixel and click should pull its weight, or it’s getting fired from the screen.

Think AI is Cool

Artificial Intelligence has already revolutionized many aspects of our lives, from self-driving cars to personalized recommendations. But we're just scratching the surface. The AI of tomorrow isn't limited by rigid programming and massive datasets. Enter Ehari, an AI designed to learn organically, think critically, and make decisions independently.

I'm building Ehari from the ground up, creating an AI that learns like a child—through experience, imagination, and simulation. Equipped with Airanex Mind, Ehari will evaluate every task and ensure it aligns with ethical guidelines. Nex Security will act as her moral compass, preventing her from taking harmful actions.

How Human think

Observe how humans, especially from different backgrounds and experiences, identify and classify everyday objects and symbols. This research explores the fascinating ways our minds process, categorize, and assign meaning to the world around us. By understanding these patterns, we can design smarter AI and more intuitive interfaces that align with natural human thinking.
